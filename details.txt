Specifications:

Three rovers are declared with names:
	rover1;
	rover2;
	rover3;

All rovers start at (0,0)

To generate obstacles, function generateObstacles() must be run with a desired number of obstacles as argument. The obstacles are generated randomly. Random and floor functions found in google with restrictions of numbers from 0 to 9 to match the board (Math.Floor(Math.random()*10).
